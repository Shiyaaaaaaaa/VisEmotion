The Empathy Box:

Visualizing Struggles

Welcome to the Empathy Box! More than just a box, it is a symbolic space that visualizes our struggles mixed with outside obstacles and inner pressures and emotions. Here, you can manipulate the length and width of the box, each representing varying levels of external obstacles and inner pressures respectively.

Emotions influence the box's material and the color of the surrounding atmosphere. Engage in this transformative experience, gain a deeper feeling of visualizing how varying conditions affect our mental states.
